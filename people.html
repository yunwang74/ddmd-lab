Professor Yun Wang

Professor, ESC - Materials and Analytical Chemistry, Griffith University

Yun Wang leads the Data-Driven Materials Design Group at Griffith University, bringing together computational modelling, data science, and experimental insight to understand and engineer functional materials for clean energy and environmental applications.

His work connects statistical mechanics, quantum chemistry, molecular simulations, and advanced materials characterisation to reveal how structure, composition, and active sites control electronic, chemical, mechanical, and thermal behaviour.

Research focus: catalysis, electrochemistry, renewable energy, environmental remediation, and advanced manufacturing.
